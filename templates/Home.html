Revival

When a policy gets lapsed if the premium is not paid within the grace period(Minimum 1 month if mode of payment is Yearly or Half-yearly or Qly and 15days if the mode is monthly).It can be revived any time within 5 years from the date of first unpaid premium. Revive the policy as soon as possible to avoid high late fines.

Loan

In case of ENDOWMENT type policy, you would be allowed to raise a loan against your policy.The essential condtion is that applicant should have paid at least 3 years of insurance premium in full.You can repay the loan with interest or continue paying the interest and allow the loan to be deducted at the time of the claim payments.

Pay Online

You can pay your premiums online by using Credit/Debit Card or Net Banking or UPI Services(like- Phonepay, GooglePay ,Paytm etc). You will get an elctronic receipt , that you should keep as soft copy or hard copy for future use. You will have to send that receipt to our e-mail for confirmation. Use this only when you have good internet signals.
